Elements

Definition and Recommended Practice

1

useLimitation

0..*

limitations on the resource’s intended use

2

accessConstraints

0..*

Codelist values identifying the type of constraints on user’s access to the resource. Most of them are not applicable to open and public data.

3

useConstraints

0..*

Codelist values identifying the type of constraints on user’s use of the resource. Most of them are not applicable to open and public data.

4

otherConstraints

0..*

This field is mandatory if codelist value = “otherRestrictions”’’ in accessConstraints or useConstraints. This is the field most commonly used for liability and disclaimer statements.
